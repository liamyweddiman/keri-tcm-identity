# Use Case: TCM Practitioner Credential Governance

## Problem
TCM practitioners face inconsistent credentialing standards across countries,
and lineage-based training is difficult to verify in modern regulatory systems.

## Solution
KERI provides a verifiable, event-based identity chain that integrates:
- Lineage  
- Training  
- Certifications  
- License issuance  
- Cross-border validation  

## Stakeholders
- Practitioners  
- Masters / lineage holders  
- Universities & hospitals  
- National medical boards  
- Patients  

## Expected Impact
- Stronger trust in TCM credentials  
- Better international mobility  
- Verified lineage for cultural preservation
