# Use Case: Herbal Supply-Chain Provenance

## Problem
Traditional medicine supply chains suffer from:
- Counterfeits  
- Mixed-origin batches  
- Lack of test transparency  
- Weak international traceability  

## Solution
KERI enables batch-level, event-based tracking from origin to pharmaceutical integration.

## Stakeholders
- Farmers  
- Processing facilities  
- Testing labs  
- Export authorities  
- Pharmaceutical manufacturers  
- Consumers  

## Expected Impact
- Better product safety  
- Clear provenance for international markets  
- Strengthened trust in traditional medicine products
